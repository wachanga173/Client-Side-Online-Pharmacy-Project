üì¶ Safe & Discreet Packaging

Temperature Control

Medications are packaged with appropriate temperature controls:

  • Ambient temperature medications: Secure boxes
  • Refrigerated items: Insulated packaging with ice packs
  • Controlled substances: Tamper-evident packaging

  • Plain, unmarked packaging (no pharmacy branding)

Package Contents

Every package includes:

  • Your medications in original, sealed containers
  • Prescription labels with dosing instructions
  • Patient information leaflets
  • Packing slip (inside package only)
  • Return instructions if needed
