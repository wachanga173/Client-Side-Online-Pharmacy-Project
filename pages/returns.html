üîÑ Returns & Refunds Overview

At Pharmacare, we understand that sometimes you may need to return a medication or request a refund. Due to the nature of pharmaceutical products and health regulations, our return policy has specific guidelines to ensure patient safety and product integrity.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Health & Safety Notice: Due to pharmaceutical regulations and patient safety requirements, not all medications can be returned once dispensed. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

‚úÖ What Can Be Returned

Eligible for Return:

  • Unopened Medications: Factory-sealed, unopened products within 30 days of delivery
  • Damaged Products: Items damaged during shipping (reported within 48 hours)
  • Incorrect Orders: Wrong medication or dosage sent by error
  • Defective Products: Manufacturing defects or quality issues
  • Over-the-Counter Items: Unopened OTC products in original packaging

Conditions for Returns:

  • Products must be in original, unopened, factory-sealed packaging
  • All labels, seals, and packaging must be intact
  • Products must not be expired or near expiration (more than 6 months remaining)
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) required
  • Items must not have been exposed to extreme temperatures

‚ùå What Cannot Be Returned

For Health & Safety Reasons, the Following Are Non-Returnable:

  • Opened Medications: Any medication that has been opened, used, or tampered with
  • Controlled Substances: Schedule II-V controlled medications (narcotics, stimulants, etc.)
  • Refrigerated Items: Temperature-sensitive medications (insulin, biologics, vaccines)
  • Compounded Medications: Custom-prepared or compounded prescriptions
  • Special Order Items: Medications ordered specifically for you
  • Expired Products: Any medication past its expiration date
  • Intimate Products: Personal health items for hygiene reasons
  • COVID-19 Test Kits: Once opened or used

Regulatory Note: These restrictions are in place to comply with pharmaceutical regulations, prevent medication tampering, and ensure patient safety.

Prescription Refunds

For prescription medications that cannot be returned but were incorrectly prescribed or have issues:

  • Contact your prescriber immediately if medication is inappropriate

  • Insurance claims: Work with your insurance provider for reimbursement
  • If we made a dispensing error, we'll arrange immediate replacement or full refund

üì¶ Damaged or Defective Items

Receiving Damaged Products

If your order arrives damaged:

  1. Do Not Use: Do not consume damaged or compromised medications
  2. Document: Take photos of the damaged packaging and product
  3. Contact Us: Report within 48 hours of delivery
  4. Inspection: Keep all packaging and product for inspection
  5. Resolution: We'll send immediate replacement or issue full refund

Manufacturing Defects

If you discover a defect after opening (unusual color, odor, or appearance):

  • Stop using the product immediately
  • Contact our pharmacy team and the product manufacturer
  • Keep the defective product and all packaging
  • We'll arrange replacement and report to regulatory authorities if needed

‚ö†Ô∏è Safety First: If you've already taken a medication that appears defective, contact your healthcare provider immediately and seek medical attention if you experience any adverse effects.
