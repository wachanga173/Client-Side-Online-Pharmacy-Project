How do I place an order?

Ordering from Pharmacare is simple:

  • Browse our product catalog or search for your medication
  • Add items to your cart
  • Proceed to checkout and create an account or log in
  • Upload your prescription if required
  • Complete payment and delivery details
  • Receive confirmation and tracking information
Do I need a prescription for all medications?

Prescription requirements vary by medication type:

  • Prescription Medications: Require a valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider
  • Over-the-Counter (OTC): Available without a prescription
  • Controlled Substances: Require additional verification and cannot be shipped to all locations

Each product page clearly indicates if a prescription is required.

How do I submit my prescription?

You can submit prescriptions in several ways:

  • Upload a photo or scan during checkout
  • Email it to prescriptions@pharmacare.com
  • Have your doctor send it directly to us
  • Mail the original to our pharmacy address

Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, PDF. Ensure all information is clearly visible.

Can I order refills?

Yes! Registered users can easily request refills:

  • Log in to your account
  • Go to "My Prescriptions"
  • Select the medication you need to refill
  • Confirm the refill request

We'll verify with your prescriber if refills remain on your prescription.

üíä Medication Safety & Information

How do I know if a medication is right for me?

Always consult with your healthcare provider before starting any medication. Our pharmacists are also available to:

  • Answer questions about medication interactions
  • Explain proper usage and dosing
  • Discuss potential side effects
  • Recommend over-the-counter alternatives

Contact our pharmacy team via phone, email, or live chat.

What if I experience side effects?

If you experience any adverse effects:

  • Severe reactions: Seek emergency medical attention immediately
  • Mild side effects: Contact your prescriber or our pharmacy
  • Report side effects to our team for documentation
  • Do not stop taking prescribed medications without consulting your doctor
How should I store my medications?

Proper storage ensures medication effectiveness:

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
  • Keep medications in original containers
  • Follow specific storage instructions on the label
  • Keep all medications out of reach of children
  • Check expiration dates regularly
  • Some medications require refrigeration - follow label instructions
